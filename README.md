# Cellar Sage

**Algorithmic wine purchasing guidance for everyday Australians.**

Cellar Sage matches your palate to real, available stock at local merchants — no guesswork, no label anxiety. An aisle assistant, not a basement ledger.

---

## What it does

1. **Palate Quiz** — 10 questions mapping your taste across Acidity, Body, Tannin, and Aromatics
2. **Algorithmic Match** — Objective scoring against a curated wine catalogue; no crowdsourced ratings
3. **Hyper-Local Sourcing** — Results ranked by proximity, with real-time stock validation for participating merchants
4. **Gastro-Match** — Food pairing logic that surfaces conflicts and proposes compromise wines before you buy
