Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Wine Wizard. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we handle your data in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and global standards.

1. Data Collection

To provide a personalized recommendation experience, we collect:

  • Palate Preferences: Responses to our onboarding quiz and adjustments made to the Palate Dial (Acidity, Body, Tannin).
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your GPS coordinates to find licensed wine merchants in your immediate vicinity (e.g., Mitcham and Greater Adelaide).
  • Wine Journal: Ratings, photos of labels, and notes you explicitly save to your profile.
  • Technical Info: Anonymous device identifiers and performance logs.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Calculate and refine your weighted palate scores.
  • Display nearby bottle shops and availability.
  • Improve the accuracy of the "Rule of 3" recommendation engine.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Mapping Services: Location data is shared with map providers to show local search results.
  • Affiliate Partners: If you purchase a wine via a link, a unique ID is shared with the merchant (e.g., Dan Murphy's) to track the referral.

4. Your Rights

Under Australian law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can wipe your account data at any time via the app settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact our development team at craigquarrell@gmail.com.
